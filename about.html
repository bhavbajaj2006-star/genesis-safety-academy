Who We Are

At Genesis Safety Academy, we are dedicated to providing world-class education and training in workplace, environmental and Fire Safety, ensuring safer environments for everyone. Also we take programs to Corporate & Industries about the importance of ESG - Environmental, Social Governance. Do an audit and submit necessary reports.

Founded in 2021, we have been in forefront of safety education, equipping professionals with the knowledge and skills to mitigate and respond effectively to emergencies. We offer off-line classes with state of art facility centrally located in Chennai, Koyambedu next to metro station & bus terminas. We also offer comprehensive training programs covering Work Place Safety Standards, Environmental hazard management, Fire Prevention & Fighting strategies with emergency evacuation and drills, Industrial Safety, Chemical Safety, Construction Safety and so on…

We cater to Student, Professionals, Unemployed youth, Housewives, businesses and organizations seeking to enhance safety practices skilling & upskilling various sections of people.

Vision Statement To be a trusted leader in cultivating a culture of safety by delivering excellence in work place, environmental and fire safety education, empowering individuals and organizations to protect lives, preserve the environment and build resilient communities.
Mission Statement Our mission is to help Students, Professionals, Workers, Unemployed Youth and Housewives by skilling and upskilling them to get employed and grow in their professional career for fulfilling their dreams.

Our mission is guided by a commitment to integrity, innovation and sustainability. We are proud to have trained over 1000+ professionals and Students contributing to safer workplaces and communities across our country.

Why Choose Genesis Safety Academy?

  • Expert Trainers: Our instructors are certified professionals with years of hands-on experience in fire safety and occupational health.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including fire prevention, workplace safety, first aid, risk management, and compliance with international safety regulations.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Learn in an environment equipped with modern tools and simulation setups that provide real-world experience.
  • Flexible Learning Options: We offer off-line, on-site, online, and hybrid training programs to suit the diverse needs of our learners.
  • Globally Recognized Certifications: Our certifications are respected worldwide, making our students highly sought after in various industries.
